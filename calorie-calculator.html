Calorie Calculator (TDEE)

Easily Calculate Calories Like A Boss!

This Calorie Calculator (TDEE) is your go-to guide to your body's energy needs! It will help you estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) – calories burned daily – based on your own measurements and activity level. Whether you need to maintain, lose, or gain weight, this calculator will provide you with a personalized calorie goal to help you reach your goals!

Here's the scoop:

  • Easy Input: Simply enter your age, gender, height in cm or inches, and weight in kg or lbs.
  • Activity Level Selection: Select one of the activity levels, from sedentary to extra active, to reasonably reflect your movement during the day.
  • Goal Selection: Select your primary goal: maintain your weight as it is currently, lose weight (by lowering your daily calorie intake), or gain weight (by raising your daily calorie intake).
  • Smart Calculation: Press the "Calculate Calories" button and the calculator uses tried and tested formulas to approximate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and then multiplies it by your activity factor to estimate your TDEE.
  • Personalized Results: You will be presented with an estimated TDEE and a recommended daily calorie intake tailored directly to your target.

Consider this your own nutrition compass, guiding you in the direction of the right calorie intake to fuel your fitness journey and guide you to the body you're working towards!
