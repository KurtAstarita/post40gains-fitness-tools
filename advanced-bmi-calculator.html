Understand Your Body Composition with This Body Fat Percentage Calculator!

This Body Fat Percentage Calculator is your calculator for estimating your body fat percentage using different methods. Being aware of your body composition can be a great way to track your fitness improvement beyond the scale reading! This calculator uses multiple formulas to give you an estimate based on your measurements.

This is how it helps you:

  • Multiple Calculation Methods: Choose from different well-known formulas:
    • Jackson/Pollock (Skinfold): Uses skinfold measurements at various body sites.
    • YMCA (Circumference): Using circumference measurements like neck, waist, and hip, and height.
    • Navy (Circumference): Also using the YMCA method, using circumference measurements.
  • Gender-Specific Calculations: To provide a more accurate estimate, calculations consider your gender.
  • Unit Selection: Enter your measurement in either centimeters/inch (for circumference) or inches/millimeters (for skinfolds), as applicable.
  • Clear Results: The calculator will estimate your body fat percentage once you enter your measurements.

Consider this a fantastic addition to your exercise toolkit that will help you better understand your body composition and monitor changes as you accomplish your goals!
